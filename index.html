What is a Pipe?

A pipw is a hollow cylindrical tube used to carry water, gas, oil, or other fluids one place to another. Pipes are the main bart of any plumbing system.

Functions of Pipes

  1. Supply clean water.

  2. Remove wastewater

  3. Transport gas or other fluids.
  4. Connect plumbing fictures such as taps, sinksm, toilets and tanks.

What is a Pipe Filling.

A pipe fitting is a component used to connect pipes, change the direction of flow, change pipe size, or close the end the end of a pipe.

Functions of Pipe Fitting.

  • Join two or more pipe.

  • Change the direction of the pipline.

  • Reduce or increase pipe size.

  • Control or stop the flow.

  • Close the end of a pipe
